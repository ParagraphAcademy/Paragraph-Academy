Privacy Policy

At Paragraph Academy, we take your privacy seriously. We are committed to protecting the personal information of our students, clients, and website visitors. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Documents for applications (like transcripts, ID copies)
  • Feedback, testimonials, or photos (with permission)

How We Use Your Information

We only use your information to:

  • Provide educational counseling and services
  • Contact you for follow-ups or updates
  • Help in applications and visa processes
  • Improve our services and support

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with any third party unless required by law or if you provide written consent (especially for universities or embassies).
