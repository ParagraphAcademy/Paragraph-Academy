GMAT Preparation

The Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT) is a standardized exam required for admission to most MBA and business school programs worldwide. Our comprehensive GMAT prep program is tailored to help you master all sections and achieve your best score.

Course Coverage

We cover all four sections of the GMAT in depth:

  • Quantitative Reasoning: Focus on arithmetic, algebra, geometry, and data analysis techniques.
  • Verbal Reasoning: Reading comprehension, critical reasoning, and sentence correction strategies.
  • Integrated Reasoning: Multi-source reasoning, graphics interpretation, and table analysis practice.
  • Analytical Writing Assessment (AWA): Essay writing techniques and sample prompts.
