IELTS

The IELTS is a globally recognized exam that measures English proficiency for academic, work, and migration purposes. It has two types: Academic, for higher education, and General Training, for work and immigration.

GRE

The GRE is an entrance test for admission to graduate and professional programs worldwide. It assesses Verbal Reasoning, Quantitative Reasoning, and Analytical Writing skills, using a computer-adaptive format to adjust question difficulty.

GMAT

The GMAT is a specialized test for admission into MBA and business-related programs. It measures Analytical Writing, Integrated Reasoning, Quantitative, and Verbal skills, using a computer-adaptive system for certain sections.

PTE

The PTE is a fully computer-based English language test designed for academic and immigration purposes. It evaluates Listening, Reading, Speaking, and Writing skills, with AI-based scoring for fairness and consistency.
